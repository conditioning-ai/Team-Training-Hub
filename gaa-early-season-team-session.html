Drill Structure
  • StartBall at Att #1 → kicked to Att #2
  • MovementAtt #1 rounds Att #2
  • Scoring RuleMin 1 more pass before they can score
📌 The scoring constraint forces the attackers to keep moving and combining — no early pot shots. The defender must stay disciplined through multiple actions.
Defensive Principle — Highest Attacker
  • Defender tracks the highest attacker (most advanced, closest to goal) — not the ball
  • Stay goal-side of the highest attacker throughout the sequence
  • Don’t be dragged to the ball — holding the highest attacker is the priority
  • By sticking to this principle, attackers run out of road and the keeper is brought into the game
🎯 Defender sticks to the highest attacker principle — the attackers run out of road and we bring the keeper into the game.
Defender: your job is the highest attacker — let the keeper deal with the ball carrier
Don’t get ball-hungry — the moment you chase the ball, the highest attacker is free
Attackers: test the principle — overload the ball side and see if the defender bites
High reps, short resets — the defender should feel the cumulative pressure across the set

Why Diamond Practices?
  • Develop close ball retention
  • Step & go — take your man on
  • Defend with the body — show away from danger
  • Repetitively, on your own and with teammates
  • Metabolically challenging
1v1 Format — Get Through a Gate
  • Numbers2–4 simultaneous 1v1s per grid
  • Work : Rest (off-season)10:10 / 15:15 / 20:20
  • Pre-season progression15:10 / 20:10 / 30:15 — fewer reps
✓ Increase intensity after pre-season by shortening rest — reduce rep count to maintain quality.
2v2 / 3v3 / 4v4 — Added Constraint
  • Same principles applyAll group formats
  • RuleAfter every pass, run through middle grid before receiving again
📌 Same principle of rules for 3v3 and 4v4. The middle grid run forces movement off the ball after every pass — no standing and waiting.
Conditioning Connector
  • Tempo RunsBetween game rounds
✓ Tempo runs bridge rounds — maintain aerobic demand without full recovery between blocks.
1v1: get your body between your man and the gate — defend with the body, not the hands
Show away from danger — force them to the outside, away from goal
2v2+: the middle grid run is non-negotiable — earn the next ball before you can receive
Tempo runs are controlled — 65–75% effort, consistent pace across all reps

Phase 07 · Pitch · No Ball
VO₂ Runs — 3:3 Intervals
~12 min
Main Work
  • VO₂ Interval Runs ▶3 sec on : 3 sec off × 3
⚡ 3:3 intervals target VO₂max stimulus — short enough to maintain quality, dense enough to accumulate a meaningful aerobic hit.
Intensity Anchor
  • Effort level90–95% max HR
  • PaceConsistent across all reps
  • Rounds×3
Finish
  • Cool downEasy jog + walk
  • MobilityHip flexors, calves, glutes
⏱ Don’t skip the cool down after VO₂ work — HR needs to come down progressively.
3 sec on is genuinely fast — treat each rep like a short max sprint
The 3 sec off is active rest — stay on your feet, stay ready
Pace fade across reps = end the set — don’t grind out sloppy reps
